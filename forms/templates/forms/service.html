Services

Nutrition counseling to pregnant and lactating mothers

Counselling about healthy eating and keeping physically active during pregnancy is recommended for pregnant women to stay healthy and to prevent excessive weight gain during pregnancy.
Promoting a healthy diet by increasing the diversity and amount of foods consumed,
Promoting adequate weight gain through sufficient and balanced protein and energy intake,
Promoting consistent and continued use of micronutrient supplements, food supplements or fortified foods.


Nutrition counseling to 0 to 6 years parents

Every parent has heard that children should be eating a " balanced diet. " But a balance of what?
The nine nutrients that every child should be getting on a daily basis: Protein , Carbohydrates, Fats, Calcium, Iron, Folate, Fiber, Vitamin A, Vitamin C.


BreastFeeding Counseling

It is important to discuss with women the importance of exclusive breastfeeding for six months and communicates with all family members about the benefits of breastfeeding for the mother and baby, the process of breastfeeding and when and how long to feed for.
Also discuss continued breastfeeding after six months and introduction of other foods in addition to breast milk.
Communicate information on the advantages of breastfeeding (including health benefits, economic benefits, etc.), to help women decide which method of feeding they will choose.


Pregnancy Club

Provides 18 group sessions on 1st 1000 days.
Taught the women about healthy pregnancy and how to raise her child healthy.
Arrange sessions for women to aware them about how to breastfeed baby,what nutrients are healthy for baby etc.


Registration of 0 to 6 years children

Registration is required for all 0-6 years children so that it becomes easy to check the status of that child in his next visit.


Takes Regular anthropometric measurements and graph plotting

The specific charts used will depend on the child's age, which determines whether the child will stand for measurement of height or lie down for measurement of length.
The measurements will be plotted on growth charts in the Boy's Growth Record or the Girl's Growth Record so that trends can be observed over time and any growth problems identified.
It is important to use the Growth Record for the correct sex since boys and girls grow to different sizes.
